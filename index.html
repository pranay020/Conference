    • Welcome To ICSEIC 2025

    We are thrilled to invite you to participate in the International Conference on Smart Electronics and Intelligent Computing (ICSEIC 2025), will be held on from May 26th to 27th, 2025, in the vibrant city of Bangalore, Karnataka, India. This exciting event is jointly organized by the School of Engineering & Technology CMR University Bangalore, University of Johannesburg in South Africa, and Manipal University Jaipur in India. ICSEIC 2025 serves as a valuable platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians, and engineers from across the globe to share their groundbreaking ideas and address the latest challenges within the field of Electronics Engineering. The conference fosters collaboration and knowledge exchange, focusing on core technological advancements in various emerging areas, including smart electronics, intelligent computing, wireless communication, RF microwave/radar, VLSI, and optical communication. We look forward to welcoming you to Bangalore for a stimulating and productive conference experience.

    Proposed: All accepted & presented papers proposed will be submitted to Springer book Series “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering (LNEE)” (https://www.springer.com/series/7818) and indexed in EI Compendex and Scopus databases.
